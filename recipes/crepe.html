Crepe Recipe:

In this recipe we will learn how to make crepes like real italinas , crepe is a doguh desert. enjoy

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1.5 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 cup of flour

how to make it:

  1. pour the eggs,milk and flour to a bowl
  2. Mix everything up very good
  3. add butter and salt
  4. Mix everything again
  5. pour thin layers of the mix on hot flat pan
  6. wait for the doguh to cook and flip to get even baked sides
  7. serve with strewberris and whipcream for maximum pleasure
