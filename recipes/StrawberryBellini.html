Strawberry Bellini Recipe:

In this recipe we will learn how to make a special strawberry cocktail , enjoy

Ingredients:

  • 3 Cups of strawberries
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon brandy
  • 1 cup sparkling wine

How To Make it :

  1. Mix the strawberries with the sugar and brandy until its mixed togther good
  2. Put in refrigerator for 30 minutes
  3. Pour sparkling wine and mix good
  4. Drink for enjoyment
