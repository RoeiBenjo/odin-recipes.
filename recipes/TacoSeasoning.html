Taco Seasoning Recipe:

In this recipe we will learn how to make the best taco seasoning you had , enjoy

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1.5 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1.5 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder

How To make:

  1. Pour everyhing to a bowl
  2. Mix everyhing togther
  3. Enjoy Your taco
